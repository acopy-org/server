acopy

copy on one device, paste on another.

acopy syncs your clipboard across all your devices in real time. text you copy on your phone shows up on your laptop instantly — and the other way around.

// install

macOS / Linux:

curl -fsSL https://acopy.org/install.sh | sh

Windows (PowerShell):

irm https://acopy.org/install.ps1 | iex

Android:

download APK from GitHub releases

installs to:
macOS: /usr/local/bin/acopy
Linux: ~/.local/bin/acopy
Windows: %LOCALAPPDATA%\acopy

then run:

acopy setup

(restart your terminal first, then verify with acopy --version)

// how it works

the client runs as a background service. it monitors your clipboard and syncs changes to all your other devices over a persistent websocket connection. clipboard data is encrypted in transit and stored only for history.

// cost

free. no ads, no tracking, no premium tier. run the public server or self-host your own.
